OCDE Better Life Index

How’s life?

What is your recipe for a better life — a good education, clean air, a nice home, money?

The OECD Better Life Index is an interactive tool that invites you to come up with your own recipe and share it with us. By creating and visualising your own index, you are able to compare some of the key factors that contribute to well-being. With your help, we can start to understand what matters most to people around the world.

What’s most important to you? Create your Better Life Index and let us know.
